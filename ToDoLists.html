10 Things to do Every Day

 

1.Read the Bible.

2.Pray (adoration, confession, thanksgiving, supplication, intercession).

3.Love others (family, friends, neighbors, and enemies).

4.Practice random acts of kindness.

5.Be Patient and Longsuffering.

6.Serve others.

7.Walk worthy of the gospel.

8.Maintain a good testimony.

9.Be careful what you say.

10.Edify one another (don’t bite and devour).
